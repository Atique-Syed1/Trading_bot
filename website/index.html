FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How is Shariah compliance determined?

We use established Islamic finance screening criteria including debt ratio, interest income, and business activity checks. Stocks are classified as Halal, Non-Halal, or Pending review.

Which exchanges are supported?

Currently we support NSE (National Stock Exchange of India) with 500+ actively traded stocks. BSE support is coming soon.

How real-time are the prices?

Prices are updated in real-time via WebSocket connection during market hours. You'll see live prices with no delay.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption. Your portfolio data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Can I use this on mobile?

Absolutely! HalalTrade Pro is a PWA (Progressive Web App) that works beautifully on mobile browsers. Install it like a native app.

How does the AI analyst work?

Our AI analyzes technical indicators, price patterns, and market sentiment to provide actionable insights and recommendations for each stock.
